Visit Lagos!

Enjoy the food, night life and culture in this vibrant and boisterous city that was formerly the Capital of Nigeria.

Top three experiences to catch when in Lagos.

Nike Art Gallery!

The gallery is one of the largest of its kind in West Africa, it boasts a collection of about 8,000 diverse artworks from various Nigerian artists.

Freedom Park Lagos

Freedom Park is a memorial and leisure park area in the middle of downtown Lagos in Lagos Island, Nigeria which was formerly Her Majesty's Broad Street Prison.

Ikeja City Mall

The Ikeja City Mall is located at Alausa in Ikeja, Lagos State. It is the first of its kind on the Mainland of Metropolitan Lagos.
